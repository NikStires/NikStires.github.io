About Me

I am currently a sophmore at Seattle University studying computer science and have been interested in programming for many years. I have taken programming courses at many different schools as well as online through programs such as Udacity. despite all of this schooling my favorite way to learn is to just hop in start problem solving.

  • Development

I love to come back to a project that I havent looked at in months and see it through a whole different lense. No matter what I am programming or what language I am using It improves my skills overall as a developer.
